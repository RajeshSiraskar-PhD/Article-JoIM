\section{Ablation Study}

This section presents the results of the ablation study conducted to evaluate the impact of various attention mechanisms on the performance of Reinforcement Learning algorithms. We analyze the general effects across all algorithms and specifically focus on the best-performing algorithms (PPO and REINFORCE). Furthermore, we detail the algorithm-specific effects for both SIT and IEEE schemas.
